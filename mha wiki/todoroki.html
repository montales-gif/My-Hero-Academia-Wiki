Todoroki

Description

Shoto Todoroki is a student at U.A. High School, born with the powerful Quirk “Half-Cold Half-Hot.” He can generate ice from his right side and fire from his left. Calm and strategic, Todoroki strives to become a hero on his own terms while reconciling with his complicated family history.

Abilities

  • Ice Generation: Creates ice walls, slides, and freezes enemies.
  • Fire Generation: Produces flames from his left side to attack or melt ice.
  • Combat Versatility: Can combine ice and fire strategically in battle.

Fun Facts

  • Todoroki is the son of Endeavor, the number two Pro Hero.
  • He initially refuses to use his fire side because of his strained relationship with his father.
  • He is known for his calm demeanor and tactical approach during fights.
