Overhaul

Description

Kai Chisaki, known as Overhaul, is the former boss of the Shie Hassaikai and one of the most dangerous villains in My Hero Academia. Cold, calculating, and obsessed with cleanliness, he believes Quirks are a “disease” that ruined society — and he is determined to cure it no matter the cost.

Using his terrifying Quirk, Overhaul can dismantle and reconstruct anything he touches, from people to the environment. This makes him a near-unstoppable close-range fighter capable of instant death or instant healing.

His emotionless personality, surgical precision, and brutality made him a nightmare for both heroes and villains, especially during his exploitation of Eri to create Quirk-erasing bullets.

Abilities

  • Overhaul (Main Quirk): Allows him to break down and rebuild anything on contact. He can heal, kill instantly, reshape terrain, or alter bodies at will.
  • Self-Fusion: Overhaul can fuse with his subordinates to greatly enhance his size, endurance, and raw power.
  • Instant Regeneration: By reconstructing his own body, he can instantly recover from severe injuries.
  • Deadly Close-Range Combat: His touch can disassemble an opponent in a split second, making him lethal at short range.
  • Environment Manipulation: He can reshape the battlefield by raising spikes, walls, and platforms from the ground.

Fun Facts

  • Overhaul has severe mysophobia and hates anything he considers “unclean.”
  • He used Eri's Rewind ability to create bullets that could erase Quirks.
  • His plague doctor-style mask reflects his obsession with disease and purity.
  • Despite being calm and quiet, he is one of the most brutal villains in the series.
