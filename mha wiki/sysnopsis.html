My Hero Academia

Discover the world of heroes, quirks, and rising champions!

Syspnosis

In a world where nearly everyone possesses superpowers known as "Quirks," society is dominated by heroes and villains. Izuku Midoriya, born without a Quirk, dreams of becoming a great hero like his idol All Might. After a fateful encounter, he inherits the legendary Quirk "One For All" and enrolls in U.A. High School, a prestigious academy training the next generation of heroes. Midoriya and his classmates face rigorous training, life-threatening challenges, and powerful villains while discovering what it truly means to be a hero.

The story blends action, comedy, and heartfelt moments as students navigate the line between personal growth and societal expectations, learning that heroism is not just about strength, but courage, compassion, and the will to protect others.
