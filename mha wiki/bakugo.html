Bakugo

Description

Katsuki Bakugo, known for his explosive personality, is a student at U.A. High School. He possesses the Quirk "Explosion," which allows him to create explosions from his sweat. Ambitious and fiercely competitive, he aims to become the number one hero.

Abilities

  • Explosion: Creates explosions from nitroglycerin-like sweat in his palms.
  • Enhanced Strength: Powerful enough to throw opponents and objects far distances.
  • Tactical Combat: Strategic fighter despite his brash attitude.

Fun Facts

  • Bakugo is childhood friends with Deku, though they have a complicated rivalry.
  • He has a fiery temper but shows care for his classmates in critical situations.
  • His hero costume is designed to enhance his explosive Quirk and mobility.
