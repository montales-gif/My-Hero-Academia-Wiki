Deku

Description

Izuku Midoriya, nicknamed Deku, is a young boy born without a Quirk in a world where almost everyone has superpowers. Despite this, he dreams of becoming a hero like his idol, All Might. Through courage and determination, he inherits the legendary Quirk "One For All" and enrolls in U.A. High School.

Abilities

  • One For All: Grants immense super strength.
  • Super Agility: High-speed movement and reflexes.
  • Enhanced Durability: Can withstand powerful attacks.
